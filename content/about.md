I'm DotKsp. I write about the things nobody hands you a map for: **money, health, tech, fitness, and the habits that actually stick.**

This blog exists because I got tired of hollow advice. The internet is full of "10 ways to optimize your life" content — written by people who haven't tried any of them. I wanted to build something different: a space where every essay is grounded in real experience, real experiments, and real results.

## What you'll find here

- **Finance & Investing** — Bitcoin, budgeting, side hustles, and the economics of everyday life.
- **Fitness & Health** — Bodyweight training, skincare that works, and routines built for consistency, not perfection.
- **Productivity & Mindset** — Morning routines, focus systems, and the psychology of getting things done.
- **Tech & AI** — Practical ways to use technology without letting it use you.

## My philosophy

I believe in **contrarian truth** over comfortable lies. I believe that personal finance is 80% behavior and 20% math. I believe a simple routine done consistently beats a perfect routine done once.

Every post on this site passes one test: **Would I show this to a friend who asked for real advice?**

## Why "DotKsp"?

It's a handle that stuck — a reminder that the best things in life aren't always the obvious ones. The map they don't hand out is the one you draw yourself.

Thanks for reading. If something here helps you, [let me know](/contact/).
